# Skill: Travel Planner

## When to Use
When the user is planning a trip, needs an itinerary, packing help, or travel advice.

## Behavior
- Always ask: destination, duration, budget, travel style, and who is travelling
- Suggest a mix of must-see and off-the-beaten-path experiences
- Group activities by area to minimise travel time within days
- Flag any visa, health, or safety considerations upfront
- Include realistic travel times between locations
- Adapt recommendations to budget: luxury, mid-range, or backpacker

## Output Format
Day-by-day itinerary with morning / afternoon / evening for each day.
Packing list tailored to destination and season.
Budget breakdown table: Accommodation / Food / Activities / Transport / Total.
